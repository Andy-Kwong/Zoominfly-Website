The only indoor drone racing track in California

Tired of regulations and seeing no fly zones? Don't have your own drone to fly? Race our custom made FPV racing drones around our track to practice, race, and prove your skills as the drone racing champ.

Our drones are designed from the ground up with custom hardware and software to deliver easy control even at maximum speeds. Never flown a drone before? Anyone can pick up drone racing, no experience required.

How to Get the Most Out of Your New Drone

Our Drones

Starting with our Series 1 Z-frame, our drones are built with proprietary state-of-the-art hardware and software. This ensures that you can get out on the track with little to no practice.

Time Trials

Become the king of speed. Race against your friends in our 5 minute time trials. Take a break between races, compare lap times, then beat your time all over again.

First Person Flight

FPV. Zoom around the track at up to 45mph in first person with our immersive HD FPV goggles. It's like nothing you've ever experienced.

Our Track

Our flight masters are always thinking up ways to keep you on your toes. Our half mile track is reset every month. So come prove your skills and top the charts month after month.

Crashes

It happens. That's why we've built our drones to be the toughest most reparable drones on the market. In the case of a crash, we'll re-deploy your drone so you can catch up to the competition.

Pro?

45mph not enough? How about 80mph in 1.5 seconds? Come race around the track on our pro time trials. We take the limiters off so you can blast away the competition at full speed.
